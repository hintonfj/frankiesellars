Teaching

Philosophy

As your teacher, I am here to assist you in reaching your musical goals. Whether that is learning a new hobby, playing in school orchestra, or taking regional and state auditions, I will guide you to improve your technique, learn new music, and learn how to practice on your own. I embrace neurodivergence and various physical abilities, and prioritize joy in every lesson, no matter your age or level. Lastly, I encourage curiosity – no question is too big to be explored!
